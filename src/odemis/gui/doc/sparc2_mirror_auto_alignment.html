Mirror auto alignment
  1. Unblank the e-beam.
  2. Select an acceleration voltage and current appropriate for the sample.
  3. Focus the SEM on the sample.
  4. Link the working distance to the current stage height.
  5. Verify the CCD has enough counts but is not saturating, adjust the exposure time and gain to obtain count rates of X - Y.
  6. Press the Auto align button.
  7. Wait until the auto alignment procedure is completed.
